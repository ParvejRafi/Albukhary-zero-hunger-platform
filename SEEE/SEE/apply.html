Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if my application is approved?

You will receive a confirmation letter with detailed instructions. Funds are usually disbursed within 2-3 weeks after approval.

Can I appeal if my application is rejected?

Yes, you may appeal or resubmit if new supporting evidence is available.

Is there a maximum amount I can request?

There is no strict limit; however, all requests are reviewed based on impact and alignment with our goals.

What if my request does not align with the focus areas?

Applications that do not align with our focus areas will be reviewed after priority applications are processed.
